Exhibition. A curated collection of projects created with Processing. New software added each month.

Curated by Filip Visnjic of CreativeApplications.net

Tech Support
by Jonah Brucker-Cohen

The year is 2020. The future of computing is a battle between two opposing forces...

Links: Coin-Operated


Base26
by Toxi

A spatial overview of four letter words in the English language.

Links: Toxi homepage, Toxi processing work, Toxi blog


Superhero
by Mikkel Koser

"To the rescue!" "I'll save you!" "You'll be safe!" "Don't worry!"

Links: Beyond Three, Processing @ Beyond Three


Cinema Redux
by Brendan Dawes

Compressing an entire film into a single image to reveal the editing rhythms and tone. Compare Vertigo, Taxi Driver, Deliverance, and more.

Links: Brendan Dawes


Version B
by Matthew Jogan

An undulating spatial construction. Click and drag the mouse to change view. Press keys 1 through 6 to change the visualization.

Links:


Bees
by Julia Stanat

Particle collisions generate a familiar structure.

Links:


Bubble Chamber
by Jared Tarbell

A generative painting system of colliding particles. Oddly familiar patterns are transcribed as a discrete universe of four particle types.

Links: Bubble Chamber, Levitated.net


Jack O Lantern
by Elise Co

Pumpkin-feeding candy game using an off-the-shelf serial LCD module.

Links: LCD modules


Sonic Wire Sculptor
by Amit Pitaru

Draw sonic sculptures with this 'rotating-table' instrument.

Links: Pitaru.com, InsertSilence


Tracking
by Robert Hodgin

Video footage of webcam interaction based on real-time tracking of light sources.

Links: Flight404


The Unbearable Lightness of Being a Pixel
by Juha Huuskonen

Stir the pixel soup with your mouse. For additional flavors, press A, S, M.

Links: Juhuu.nu


Ricochet
by Josh Nimoy, Mark Napier

Springy text collides with the edge of the image to jumble the forms. Press keys on your keyboard to generate new letters.

Links: JTNimoy, Potatoland
