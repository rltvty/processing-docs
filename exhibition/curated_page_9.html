Exhibition. A curated collection of projects created with Processing. New software added each month.

Curated by Filip Visnjic of CreativeApplications.net

YMYI
by Joao Martinho Moura and Jorge Sousa

Space where the user interacts with a living image through his body movements.

Links: Joao Martinho Moura, Master in Technology and Digital Art


Body Navigation
by Jonas Jongejan and Ole Kristensen for Recoil Performance Group

Infrared motion tracking and floor projection installation for a Danish Dance Theatre production with choreographer Tina Tarpgaard.

Links: Ole Kristensen, HalfdanJ, Recoil Performance Group


News Knitter
by Ebru Kurbak & Mahir M. Yavuz

Visualization of news feeds as knitted garments.

Links: Mahir M. Yavuz


Wordscapes
by Peter Cho

A collection of reactive one-word poem landscapes.

Links: Typotopo.com, Pcho.net


Counterclockwise
by David Muth

A generative video piece with music by Hiaz.

Links: DavidMuth.net


proximityOfNeeds
by Lia

Generative, parameterized images and sound.

Links: strangethingshappen.org


Volume of Emptiness
by John Houck

Volume of Emptiness is an installation that continually unfolds architectural spaces. Virtual volumes are created by a field of motors in the ceiling that each have a number of threads attached to them.

Links: JohnHouck.com


Big Screens
by NYU ITP

Dan Shiffman's NYU students write software for a 120 by 12 foot video wall at IAC's world headquarters designed by Frank Gehry.

Links: Shiffman.net


Nervous System, Play + Learn
by Jessica Rosenkrantz and Jesse Louis-Rosenberg

Shows the global exchange of information by visualizing volumes of data flowing in and out of New York City.

Links: Nervous System


NYTE
by SENSEable City Lab

Shows the global exchange of information by visualizing volumes of data flowing in and out of New York City.

Links: SENSEable City Lab, AaronKoblin.com


Cascade on Wheels
by Steph Thirion et al.

Two visualizations of traffic patterns in Madrid.

Links: Transparent


MYPOCKET
by Burak Arikan

Discloses the artist's personal financial records by exploring and revealing essential patterns in daily transactions.

Links: Burak-Arikan.com
